★ THE MISSING ABSTRACTION LAYER

One representation for
every energy model.

Text has the token. Energy ML had nothing — until EnergyGraph. Four composable objects encode the physics that lets any model's inputs and outputs interoperate, and weights transfer.

PhysicsSpec
PDE class, boundary conditions and inferred terms — the basis for weight transfer.
SensorGraph
Wells and instruments as nodes; physical relationships as weighted edges.
FieldState
A discretised field snapshot with per-node calibrated uncertainty.
MaterialSpec
Composition, crystal graph and operating conditions for materials GNNs.

HOW IT WORKS

Checkpoint to running model,
in four steps.

No model reaches the public zoo until the physics constraint checker passes.
01
Upload
Drag a .pt or .safetensors checkpoint — TorchPharma format accepted.
02
Physics check
Automated validation of PDE residual, conservation and calibration.
03
Model card
A formal physics-domain card: provenance, performance, safety class.
04
Infer & fine-tune
Call it from the SDK, REST API or fine-tune in the browser on your field.
# Python SDK
from energyhub import EnergyModel
model = EnergyModel.from_hub("tsuma/olkaria-inverse-pinn-v1.2")
result = model.invert(sensor_graph=my_field)
